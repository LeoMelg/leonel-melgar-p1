Our mission

Every year, thousands of pets across the united states are abandoned or abused. The reasons for this vary, but yet we can all agree that these animals that can't fend for themselves deserve a better life. Here at Shelter for Helpers our mission is to change the life of an animal that has suffered in any sort of way.

Our Beginnigs

We were founded in Milwaukee, by a dashing young man with big aspirations and an even bigger heart. Over the course of the years, we have expanded to multiple states. In the future we hope to expand to all of the country and branch out to other countries. Our main target for expansion is Latin America where there is a big need.

How you can help

There are various ways to help out our organizaation. Some of the stuff includes...

  • Donating money
  • Donating food/toys/beds/etc
  • Spreading the word
  • Following us on social media
Donations + Benefits
For 5 dollars we are able to provide one animal a new toy or rawhide bone. Ten dollars helps us get 2 animals new toys or a collar. Twenty dollars helps us bring a new bed to one of our animals at our shelter. Fifty dollars helps buy a big bag of food to feed the animals in our facilities. Depending on whatever amount you were kind enough to donate it will be used as best as it can accordingly.
